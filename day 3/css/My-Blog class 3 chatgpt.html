TYPES

WHITE HAT

A white hat hacker, also known as an ethical hacker, is a cybersecurity professional who uses their hacking skills to identify and fix security vulnerabilities in computer systems with the owner's permission.

BLACK HAT

A black hat hacker is an individual who illegally accesses computer systems and networks with malicious intent, often for financial gain, revenge, or to cause disruption.

GRAY HAT

A gray hat hacker is a computer security expert or hacker who operates outside of the traditional ethical boundaries of white hat hackers (who work with permission to improve security) and the malicious intent of black hat hackers (who exploit systems for personal gain or harm).
