GYM

DIET

A gym diet focuses on providing the body with the necessary nutrients to support workouts, muscle growth, and overall health. It typically includes a balanced intake of protein, carbohydrates, healthy fats, and essential vitamins and minerals, with an emphasis on hydration.

Exercise

A gymnasium (or gym) is a place for physical exercise, typically equipped with various machines, weights, and other equipment. Common gym exercises include strength training (like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses), cardio (like running on a treadmill or cycling), and flexibility exercises (like stretching and yoga).
